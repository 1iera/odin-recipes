Delicious Mexican Rice Soup

This vegan Mexican Rice Soup is perfect for colder days so it can warm up your family.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups low-sodium vegetable stock
  • 1 15-ounce can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 15-ounce can fire-roasted tomatoes
  • 1 cup long grain brown rice, toasted

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan, saut√© the onion and garlic in 1 tablespoon low-sodium vegetable stock or water over medium-high heat for 7 minutes.
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients, reduce heat to low, and simmer for 25-30 minutes.
  3. Season to taste
  4. Serve with toppings of your choice, or the ones listed above
